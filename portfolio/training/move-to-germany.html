Munich and Frankfurt offer the highest salaries, while Berlin offers great opportunities at slightly lower rates. German salaries include excellent benefits like 25-30 vacation days, healthcare, and strong job security.

Germany has exceptional work-life balance. The Arbeitszeitgesetz (Working Hours Act) limits work to 48 hours per week maximum. Most tech companies offer:

  • 35-40 hour work weeks
  • 25-30 paid vacation days
  • Sick leave without questions
  • Strict boundaries - no emails after hours!

German culture prioritizes efficiency over long hours, making it ideal for sustainable career growth.

1) EU Blue Card (most popular) - for skilled workers with job offers

The process has been streamlined significantly in 2025 with faster processing times.
